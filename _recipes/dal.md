### Ingredients (Serves 4)

- 1 cup Toor dal (or toor dal + moong dal)
- 1 onion
- 1 tomato
- 0.25 tsp Haldi
- 0.75 tsp Dhanya powder
- 0.75 tsp Jeera powder

Vaghar:

- 2-3 tbsp oil
- 7-8 flakes of garlic (or garlic paste)
- 1.5 tsp Red chilli powder
- Curry leaves
